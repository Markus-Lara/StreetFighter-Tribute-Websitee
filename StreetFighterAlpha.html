Street Fighter Alpha (1995–1998)

The Alpha series bridges the story between the original Street Fighter and Street Fighter II, with a bold anime art style, faster movement, and deeper combo and super systems.

Major Innovations

  • Super Combos, custom fighting styles, and dramatic Battle mechanics.
  • Air blocking, Alpha/Chain combos, and smoother, more fluid animation.

Classic Moments & Lore

  • Focus on younger versions of Ryu, Ken, Chun‑Li, and others.
  • Akuma and M. Bison become central figures in the saga’s storyline.
