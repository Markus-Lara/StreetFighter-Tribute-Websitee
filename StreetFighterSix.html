Street Fighter 6 (2023–Present)

Street Fighter 6 opens a new chapter with the Drive System, an open‑world World Tour mode, and a graffiti‑inspired art style that brings veterans and newcomers together in the next generation of fighting games.

Major Innovations

  • Drive System (Drive Impact, Drive Parry, Drive Rush, Drive Reversal, Overdrive Arts) built around a shared Drive Gauge.
  • World Tour single‑player RPG‑style mode with a customizable avatar.
  • Battle Hub for social play, tournaments, and online matchmaking.
  • Bold graffiti‑inspired UI and modern/Classic control schemes.

Classic Moments & eSports

  • Main game of the Capcom Pro Tour and Capcom Cup in the SF6 era.
  • Strong competitive scene streamed on official Capcom channels.
