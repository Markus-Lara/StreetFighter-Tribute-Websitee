Street Fighter (1987)

Released in 1987, Street Fighter I introduced the world to head-to-head arcade battles and the iconic Ryu/Ken duo, though the game itself was not widely popular at release. Its tough controls and limited gameplay kept it from making a big impact—Street Fighter didn’t truly become a classic until the release of Street Fighter II, which launched the franchise into global fame.

Ryu and Ken were the only characters playable!

Iconic moves like the Hadouken (Known as fire Ball) and Shoryuken (Known as uppercut).
