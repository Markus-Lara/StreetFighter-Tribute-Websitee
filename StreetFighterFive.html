Street Fighter V (2016–2020)

Street Fighter V introduced the V‑System, strong online play, and a live‑service model with seasons of new content, keeping the game active in tournaments and casual play for years.

Major Innovations

  • V‑System (V‑Skills, V‑Triggers, V‑Reversals, later V‑Shift) to customize each character’s tools.
  • Robust online multiplayer with cross‑platform play and ranked/casual modes.
  • Ongoing seasonal updates with new stages, costumes, and balance changes.

Classic Moments & Competitive Impact

  • Anchor of the Capcom Pro Tour and many major eSports events for multiple years.
  • Champion Edition collected nearly all content into one definitive version.
